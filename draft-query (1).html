What each dimension measures

  • Answer Structure — Does the piece open with a clear, direct answer to an implied question? Are key facts surfaced early rather than buried in the body?
  • Semantic Clarity — Are entities (teams, players, events, stats) named explicitly and unambiguously? Could an AI parse this without contextual knowledge?
  • Claim Specificity — Are assertions backed by concrete figures, dates, names, or sourced data rather than vague qualifiers?
  • Quotability — Does the piece contain discrete, self-contained passages that could surface as featured snippets or AI summaries?
  • Heading Hierarchy — Is the structure scannable? Are subheads descriptive enough to stand alone as mini-answers?
